Jit Jots

A non-profit organization founded by University of British Columbia students.

Mission Statement

Our non-profit mission is to share our passion for science to young, curious minds. To achieve this, we create unique weekly activity sheets that cater to elementary school students. Correspondingly, we host workshops to explore the topics that our worksheets have covered via demonstrations, interactive quizzes, and experiments. We hope that through our service, we can bring kids in our communities together, united by a common interest in science!

Meet the Team

Meet the Jit Jots team. We are University of British Columbia Science students who were inspired about our learning enough to share our passion to the world!
