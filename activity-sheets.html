Activity Sheets


Jit Jots activity sheets contain educational yet fun activities with the aim of teaching elementary school students various educational topics.


Jit Jots activity pages are categorized as either primary or intermediate. The primary level activity sheets are intended for children in grades K-3 and the intermediate level activity sheets are intended for children in grades 4-7.
